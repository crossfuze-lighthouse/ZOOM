Frequently asked questions
When should a rep start using ZoomInfo actively?

After licence allocation, activation, profile setup, and signals basics are complete.

What is the role of intent and signals?

To improve prioritisation and outreach relevance, not to justify broad untargeted activity.

Why create target account alerts?

To surface timely prompts on accounts you genuinely want to act on.

What matters more: volume or relevance?

Relevance. The objective is better signal-led activity, not just more output.
